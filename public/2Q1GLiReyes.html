About the Team !!

The boys' volleyball team has a long-standing reputation as a powerhouse, having participated in the Spring Tournament for three consecutive years and a total of 31 times overall.
They were the runners-up in the last Interhigh competition and are currently the second seed.
The team boasts a roster of skilled players, including the Miya Brothers, as well as Rintarō Suna and Aran Ojiro,
who are the top two scorers for Inarizaki. Aran Ojiro is recognized as one of the top five aces in the nation.

Inarizaki has earned a reputation as the "strongest challengers" due to their unconventional playing
style and willingness to take risks. Their motto, "We don't need (things like) memories,"
emphasizes their commitment to not being weighed down by past achievements and always embracing new challenges.
